# Product Requirements Document

## Product Name

UPI Split Payments

## Author

Product Manager

---

## Background

UPI transactions in India have become the dominant payment method for peer-to-peer and merchant payments. However, shared expenses still require one user to make the full payment and manually collect money from others.

Common use cases include:

* Restaurant bills
* Group travel
* Fuel expenses
* Grocery purchases
* Shared subscriptions

This creates friction, delayed settlements, and poor user experience.

---

## Problem Statement

Users frequently incur shared expenses but lack a seamless way to divide payments at the point of purchase.

Current flow:

1. One person pays.
2. Others repay later.
3. Repayments are delayed or forgotten.

This increases user effort and reduces transaction transparency.

---

## Goal

Enable users to split merchant payments directly through UPI while ensuring merchants receive full payment instantly.

## User Stories

### Student

As a student, I want to split restaurant bills instantly so I don't need to chase friends for repayments.

### Traveler

As a traveler, I want trip expenses automatically divided among participants.

### Family User

As a family member, I want recurring household expenses split transparently.

---

## MVP Features

### Equal Split

Automatically divide total bill among selected users.

### Custom Split

Allow users to assign different contribution amounts.

### UPI Collect Requests

Generate repayment requests instantly.

### Due Tracker

Track pending and completed settlements.

### Notifications

Send reminders for unpaid requests.
