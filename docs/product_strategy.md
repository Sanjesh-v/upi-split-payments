# Product Strategy

## Market Opportunity

UPI processes billions of transactions monthly. Group payments remain underserved despite frequent usage across dining, travel, entertainment, and household spending.

Split Payments can increase transaction frequency and user engagement while improving payment convenience.

---

## Target Users

### Primary Segment

Young professionals aged 22–35.

### Secondary Segment

College students.

### Tertiary Segment

Families and roommates.

---

## Product Positioning

"Pay together, instantly."

The product removes friction from shared payments and provides instant transparency.

---

## Key Assumptions

* Users prefer settling expenses immediately.
* Merchants want guaranteed payment settlement.
* Repayment reminders improve completion rates.

---

## Growth Strategy

### Stage 1

Launch in restaurant category.

### Stage 2

Expand into travel and entertainment.

### Stage 3

Enable recurring shared payments.

---

## Revenue Model

### Merchant Processing Fee

0.2% of transaction value.

### Premium Features

Advanced expense tracking.

### Credit Products

Pay Later functionality.

---

## KPI Framework

Acquisition

* New Split Users
* Adoption Rate

Engagement

* Split Transactions Per User
* Average Group Size

Retention

* Monthly Active Split Users

Financial

* Transaction Volume
* Merchant Revenue

Operational

* Settlement Time
* Completion Rate

---

## Recommendations

1. Prioritize restaurant use cases.
2. Incentivize first-time split payments.
3. Reduce settlement time through reminders.
4. Launch merchant partnerships in major cities.
5. Introduce AI recommendations after achieving scale.
